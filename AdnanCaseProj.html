Case Overview

Adnan Syed was convited of the murder of his ex girlfriend Hae Min Lee

Case Facts

  • Victim: Hae Min Lee, 18-year-old high school student
  • Defendant: Adnan Syed, ex boyfriend of Hae and Cristina Gutierrez
  • Date of Crime: January 13, 1999
  • Location: Baltimore, Maryland
  • Legal Outcome: Convicted February 25th, 2000

Case Timeline

January 13, 1999

Hae Min Lee disappears after school.

February 9, 1999

Lee's body is found in Leakin Park.

February 28, 1999

Adnan Syed is arrested and charged.

February 25, 2000

Syed is convicted of first-degree murder.

Case Evidence

Key evidence presented in the case:

Witnesses

Testimony from Jay Wilds stating that Adnan is guilty of the murder.

Cell Records

Cell tower data locating Adnan near burial site.

Hae Min Lee’s Car Location

Her car was found abandoned in a lot that Jay led police to. Meaning Jay has inside info on the case and is a witness.

Lack of Defense

Defendent wouldn't look into Adnan's call records nor test Hae's corpse for DNA.

Unreliable Cell Tower Evidence

Incoming call data used to place Adnan near Leakin Park could be unreliable.

Asia as Alibi

Asia states that she saw Adnan at the library at the time of Hae’s disappearance. Adnan’s defense never contacted her though.
